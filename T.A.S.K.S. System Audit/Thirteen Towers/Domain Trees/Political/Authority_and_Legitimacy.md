# Authority and Legitimacy Tree

## Recognized Voice (Novice)
**Trigger:** Be consulted by local leaders on important decision
**Effect:** +1 Edge when claiming authority in your domain
**Acts:** "Local expert" → "Resistance advisor" → "Recognized leader"

## Mandate Holder (Journeyman)
**Trigger:** Receive formal authority from established institution
**Effect:** +2 Edge exercising institutional authority. Your commands are obeyed.
**Acts:** "Guild master" → "Rebellion officer" → "Appointed coordinator"

## Legitimacy Crafter (Adept)
**Trigger:** Create or restore legitimate authority where it was absent
**Effect:** +2 Edge establishing new legitimate structures. Transfer authority.
**Acts:** "Establish local council" → "Legitimate resistance" → "Found new institutions"

## The Anointed (Expert)
**Trigger:** Multiple factions recognize your authority independently
**Effect:** +3 Edge all exercises of authority. Your legitimacy is unquestioned.
**Acts:** "Regional authority" → "Rebellion legitimacy" → "Planetary mandate"

## Sovereign Architect (Apex)
**Trigger:** Create new form of legitimate authority accepted across cultures
**Effect:** +4 Edge all authority. You define what legitimacy means.
**DISCOVERS:** Legitimacy flows from unexpected sources when needed. (GM: AF64/Monuments amplifying worthy authority.)
