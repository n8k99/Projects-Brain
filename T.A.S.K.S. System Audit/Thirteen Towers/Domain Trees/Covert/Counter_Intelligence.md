# Counter-Intelligence Tree
## Detecting and Neutralizing Enemy Intelligence Operations

## Spy Catcher (Novice)
**Trigger:** Identify and expose an infiltrator in your organization
**Effect:** +1 Edge spotting surveillance and planted agents.
**Acts:** "Find scholar-spy" → "Expose dragon agent" → "Detect Monument monitoring"

## Pattern Breaker (Journeyman)
**Trigger:** Successfully feed false information to enemy intelligence
**Effect:** +2 Edge detecting intelligence operations. Can plant disinformation.
**Acts:** "Mislead rival scholars" → "Feed false intel to dragons" → "Spoof Tower sensors"

## Network Breaker (Adept)
**Trigger:** Dismantle an enemy intelligence cell without alerting its handlers
**Effect:** +2 Edge mapping enemy networks. Turn enemy agents with +1 Edge.
**Acts:** "Break spy ring" → "Flip dragon cultists" → "Subvert AF64 protocols"

## Counterspy Master (Expert)
**Trigger:** Run successful double-agent operation for 5+ sessions
**Effect:** +3 Edge all counter-intelligence. Protect your networks from infiltration.
**Acts:** "Master of deception" → "Counter dragon intelligence" → "Deceive T.A.S.K.S. monitoring"

## The Watcher of Watchers (Apex)
**Trigger:** Detect and neutralize intelligence operation targeting you personally
**Effect:** +4 Edge counter-intelligence. Know when you're being watched. Can feed specific narratives to watchers.
**DISCOVERS:** The best watchers sometimes seem to approve of your work. (GM: AF64 evaluating approvingly.)
