## Quick Reference

**Rolling**: 1d20 + Stat Mod + Edge vs Difficulty
- **Difficulty 8**: Trivial | **12**: Standard | **16**: Hard | **20+**: Extreme

**Position**: Controlled (low risk) | Risky (real consequences) | Desperate (severe)

**Effect**: Limited (partial) | Standard (normal) | Great (major)

**Edge Sources**: Archetype + Domain + Tree Nodes + Situation = ONE number (-5 to +5)

**Resolve Uses**: Push through, resist mental pressure, activate special abilities
