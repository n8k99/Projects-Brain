# Overview

[[Korrallan Bravone]] sets out from the mines beneath the [[Zhulmar Dominion]] which he has called home his whole life. He seeks to make a name for himself, as well as redeem his family's name through the unlikely journey of heroic proportions.

# Arc Timeline

- **Parent Epic:** [[Blood on The Infernal Route]]
- **Adventure Sequence:** [[Prophesy of The Mark]] ‚Üí [[The Wailing Steppes]] ‚Üí [[Inevitability]]
- **Temporal Span:** This covers the three year span leading up to [[The Grand Masquerade]].
- **Geographic Focus:** [[High Nook Valley]], [[Zhulmar Dominion]], [[Coalition of Etrivalian Tribes]]
- **Level Range:** 1 ‚Üí 9
- **Tone:** Heroic with Political overtones

# Thematic Core

- **Central Theme:** Redemption through courage
- **Player Questions:** What is the cost of ambition? Can you trust unlikely allies?
- **Mythic Reflection:** Mirrors the rise of [[Orbis]]‚Äô heroes during times of political upheaval

- **The Ember Trial and Ceremony of Eviction:** Korrallan is exiled from the mines of Hatfield, forcing him to leave his home and begin his journey. - **Meeting SylEshis, Time Wyrmling:** Korrallan learns he exists across multiple worldtracks and plays pivotal roles in many timelines. Impact: Transforms his understanding of destiny and free will, elevating his personal quest to cosmic significance.
- **Carving the Roadmarker of Sundering:** Korrallan fulfills the prophecy by carving his sigil into the ancient monument. Impact: Marks the transition from Act I to Act II; validates his heroic journey and attracts attention from major factions.
- **Trial of the Four Winds:** Korrallan and party endure ritual trials representing East (Z√ºg), South (√ñmn√∂), West (Baruun), and North (Hoid) winds. Impact: Earns tribal recognition, forms political alliances with Etrivalian tribes, and proves worthiness for greater challenges.

## Outcomes and Legacy

- **Success:** Korrallan prevents the assassination at The Grand Masquerade, securing his father's life and stabilizing the political landscape. The Bravone family name is redeemed and Korrallan is offered a diplomatic position bridging Zhulmar Dominion and Etrivalian Tribes. MalLaoth is defeated, his hoard claimed, and his territories opened for settlement. The party gains Aria's Diplomatic Sword and recognition from ZyxDrapip. Strong alliance with Etrivalian tribes through Trial of the Four Winds completion. Knowledge of the multiverse from SylEshis positions party for future cosmic-scale adventures. Consortium of Whispers' machinations are partially exposed, limiting their influence.
- **Partial Success:** The assassination is thwarted but with casualties among political figures, creating power vacuums and instability. Korrallan's reputation is mixed‚Äîheroic to some factions, reckless to others. MalLaoth escapes with his life but is driven from his territory, creating a vengeful recurring antagonist. Some but not all trials of the Four Winds are completed, resulting in conditional tribal support. Partial information about the Consortium is revealed but key operatives remain hidden. Aria's Diplomatic Sword remains unclaimed or is damaged in acquisition. The prophecy is fulfilled but its "deeper meaning" remains cryptic.
- **Failure:** The assassination succeeds, plunging the Zhulmar Dominion into succession crisis and regional war. Korrallan is blamed for the failure, his family name further tarnished. MalLaoth's ambush succeeds, decimating tribal leadership and disrupting the Trial of the Four Winds. The Etrivalian Tribes fracture without their Emperor's guidance. The party is forced to flee through Khuragz portal, pursued by multiple factions. The Consortium of Whispers capitalizes on the chaos, securing ancient technology and expanding influence. Korrallan's multiverse significance revealed by SylEshis becomes a burden as multiple timeline versions face similar failures. Regional instability escalates into full-scale conflict between Luminarian Empire and Zhulmar Dominion.
- **World Impact:** The arc establishes Korrallan as a figure of regional importance whose actions ripple across timelines. Dragon politics shift as young dragons like MalLaoth are inspired or warned by the outcome. The relationship between surface dwellers and dragon overlords enters a new phase. The Portal Network's strategic importance becomes widely recognized. The Consortium of Whispers either suffers setbacks or gains significant advantages in their search for the Four Shards. The Coalition of Etrivalian Tribes becomes either a stabilizing force or collapses into warring factions. The prophecy's fulfillment (or failure) at the Roadmarker of Sundering becomes a touchstone for future prophetic interpretation across Orbis.
