### The Extinction of the Gold Dragons

When [[Aurenstraal The Dawnwing Ascendant]] departed from the Council of Ancients to pursue ascension beyond the material plane, he left his [[T.A.S.K.S. ## Agendas for All

### Opposing Visions for Orbis

At the heart of the Council's current divide are two fundamentally opposing visions for the future of Orbis and its inhabitants, represented by two ancient dragons whose philosophies have shaped the Council's politics for millennia.

**[[IgnarathTheScorchedTyrant]]** seeks to return to Orbis by systematically eliminating any metallic dragons who would oppose his supremacy. His grand strategy is twofold:

1. **Trigger humanoid wars** across the continent that will:
   - Distract the metallic, gem, and exotic dragons who would otherwise unite against him
   - Set off a massive reduction in humanoid populations without requiring another direct dragon-led cull
   - Create power vacuums that his chromatic allies can exploit

2. **Restore chromatic dominance** by:
   - Continuing the systematic elimination of metallic dragons (as demonstrated by the gold dragon genocide)
   - Establishing chromatic dragons as the unquestioned rulers of their respective territories
   - Eventually returning from exile to claim his place as the supreme dragon of Orbis

In stark contrast, **[[VelthorynTheSilverSentinel]]** advocates for a fundamentally different relationship between dragons and humanoids. His vision is to:

1. **Empower humanoid civilizations** to:
   - Develop their full potential without draconic interference
   - Create societies based on justice, knowledge, and balance
   - Eventually become true partners with dragonkind rather than subjects or pawns

2. **Minimize draconic influence** by:
   - Limiting direct dragon intervention in humanoid affairs
   - Creating systems where dragons serve as advisors rather than rulers
   - Establishing boundaries between dragon territories and humanoid realms

### Council Alignments

The remaining Council members align themselves along this philosophical spectrum, with some firmly in one camp and others taking more nuanced positions:

#### Aligned with Ignarath's Vision
- **[[SylvrathTheRootfather]]** - Seeks to reclaim territories lost to humanoid expansion and restore primeval forests under chromatic control
- **[[VorthyxTheWardenOfStorms]]** - Desires to dominate coastal regions through strategic weather manipulation and naval warfare
- **[[Chemraza the Venombinder]]** - Works to corrupt political structures through subtle manipulation, creating fractured societies vulnerable to draconic rule
- **[[NymalgraMistressOfSolitude]]** - Maintains isolation of northern territories, believing dragons should rule absolutely in their domains

#### Aligned with Velthoryn's Vision
- **[[MaltherionArbiterOfDragonkind]]** - Upholds ancient laws and treaties between dragons and humanoids, serving as mediator and judge
- **[[Kaerinth Keeper Of Forgotten Truths]]** - Preserves knowledge and history for all species, believing shared wisdom leads to harmony

#### Complex/Neutral Positions
- **[[ZytharaDreamwalkerOfTheAmethystVeil]]** - Observes the conflict through visions and dreams, rarely taking direct action but offering cryptic guidance to both sides
- **[[Ithryzkal Mindvault Of The Sapphire Nexus]]** - Studies the psionic patterns of the conflict, believing the mental evolution of both dragons and humanoids should proceed without interference
- **[[Droskithar Devourer Of Leylines]]** - Concerned primarily with magical energies, intervenes only when the conflict threatens to destabilize Orbis' magical infrastructure
- **[[OrmyraMotherOfTides]]** - Remains largely neutral, focused on oceanic domains but growing increasingly concerned about how the conflict might affect aquatic realms

### The Broken Accord

The current escalation of tensions‚Äîmanifesting in the events now known as "The Broken Accord"‚Äîrepresents the most direct implementation of Ignarath's strategy in centuries. By manipulating the [[Government Types/FactionsandPolitics/Consortium of whispers/Consortium of Whispers]] and other proxy organizations, Ignarath has engineered a political situation that threatens to erupt into continental war.

Velthoryn and his allies recognize this strategy but find themselves at a disadvantage following the extinction of the gold dragons. Their counter-strategy focuses on identifying and supporting key humanoid agents who might navigate the complex political landscape and prevent the worst outcomes of Ignarath's machinations.

#### The Panopicon Gambit

In a move of unprecedented desperation, **[[VelthorynTheSilverSentinel]]** has recently established secret communication with the crews of the [[EmColonizationFleet]]‚Äîadvanced observers who have maintained a hidden presence known as **[[The Panopticon]]** for several thousand years. These watchers have remained in strict observational mode, documenting but never interfering with Orbis' development.

Velthoryn has begun pleading with these ancient visitors to emerge from their long isolation and tip the balance in favor of humanoid civilizations. This controversial approach has not been shared with most Council members, as it represents a fundamental break with the ancient compact that has governed draconic affairs for millennia.

If successful, this gambit could introduce an entirely new power to the equation‚Äîone with knowledge and capabilities beyond even the most ancient dragons. However, it also risks unforeseen consequences that could permanently alter the nature of Orbis itself.

The coming conflict will determine not just the political boundaries of humanoid nations, but the fundamental relationship between dragons and humanoids for generations to come.

## Aftermath of the Purge
Several gold-lineage ancients who had advocated for cautious engagement with the Luminarian Empire were killed or driven from Orbis during the chromatic-led purge that followed revelations about Na-era technology. The loss of these voices shifted the Council's balance toward more hardline anti-technological positions and remains a primary source of schism between metallic and chromatic factions today.
