# Military Power Tree
## Force Projection and Command

This tree represents your ability to lead military forces, understand tactics, and project power through organized violence.

## Street-Ready Fighter (Novice)

**Advancement Trigger:** Win 3 serious fights, proving you can handle yourself in violence

**Mechanical Effect:** +1 Edge in personal combat. Know basic weapon use and fighting tactics.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Capable warrior recovering old fighting techniques"
- Act 4: "Rebellion fighter, combat-ready"
- Act 5: "Military operative with combat training"

---

## Skirmish Leader (Journeyman)

**Advancement Trigger:** Successfully lead a small group (5-10) through combat engagement

**Mechanical Effect:** +2 Edge commanding small units. Can coordinate tactics for squads.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Lead town militia or adventuring party"
- Act 4: "Lead resistance cell in combat"
- Act 5: "Squad leader for fleet operations"

---

## Tactics on the Ground (Adept)

**Advancement Trigger:** Win battle through tactical superiority, not just force

**Mechanical Effect:** +2 Edge in tactical planning. Read battlefield, predict enemy movements, exploit terrain.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Study ancient battle techniques"
- Act 4: "Outmaneuver superior forces"
- Act 5: "Apply proven tactics at fleet scale"

---

## Warband Captain (Expert)

**Advancement Trigger:** Command 50+ troops successfully across multiple engagements

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge commanding company-scale forces. Can coordinate multiple units, manage morale, handle complex battlefield situations.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Lead regional military forces"
- Act 4: "Command rebellion military wing"
- Act 5: "Company commander in fleet marines"

---

## Logistics-Minded Commander (Expert)

**Advancement Trigger:** Win campaign through superior logistics and supply management

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge understanding military logistics. Know that armies march on their stomachs. Coordinate supply with combat operations.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Organize regional defense efficiently"
- Act 4: "Keep rebellion forces supplied and mobile"
- Act 5: "Fleet logistics and supply operations"

---

## Regiment Commander (Master)

**Advancement Trigger:** Successfully command 500+ troops in sustained campaign

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge commanding regiment-scale forces. Coordinate multiple companies, manage officers, handle strategic objectives.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "High-ranking military leader"
- Act 4: "Rebellion army commander"
- Act 5: "Regiment commander in fleet forces"

---

## Theater Tactician (Master)

**Advancement Trigger:** Plan and execute multi-engagement campaign across region

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge theater-level strategy. Coordinate multiple regiments, manage fronts, think beyond individual battles.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Strategic military mind"
- Act 4: "Plan rebellion campaigns"
- Act 5: "Fleet theater operations"

---

## Political Soldier (Master)

**Advancement Trigger:** Navigate military-political interface successfully, serve political objectives without compromising military effectiveness

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge understanding military politics. Know when military force serves political ends, when to refuse impossible orders, how to work with civilian leadership.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Bridge between nobles and military"
- Act 4: "Rebellion military-political coordinator"
- Act 5: "Fleet political-military liaison"

---

## Army-Level Command (Apex)

**Advancement Trigger:** Command multiple regiments (5,000+ troops) across sustained campaign with realm-scale stakes

**Mechanical Effect:** +4 Edge commanding armies. Coordinate with other army commanders, manage theater strategy, work with political leadership at highest levels.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Supreme military commander for realm"
- Act 4: "Rebellion armed forces commander"
- Act 5: "Fleet army commander"

---

## Realm General / Warlord (Apex)

**Advancement Trigger:** Your military decisions shape the fate of realms

**Mechanical Effect:** +4 Edge all military operations. Your reputation precedes you. Enemies and allies plan around your involvement.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Legendary general of the age"
- Act 4: "The military mind of the rebellion"
- Act 5: "Fleet admiral or supreme military commander"

**DISCOVERS:** War is about logistics, morale, and political will more than combat prowess. The best generals prevent wars or win them before fighting starts.
