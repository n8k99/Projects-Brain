# Command Authority Tree
## Legitimate Military and Administrative Authority

This tree represents formal authority to command, give orders, and have them obeyed. It's about institutional legitimacy, not just martial skill.

## Known Quantity (Novice)

**Advancement Trigger:** Gain formal recognition from military or administrative institution

**Mechanical Effect:** +1 Edge when exercising official authority. People recognize you have legitimate backing.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Recognized officer or official"
- Act 4: "Rebellion officer with formal rank"
- Act 5: "Fleet commissioned officer"

---

## Recognized Officer / Steward (Journeyman)

**Advancement Trigger:** Receive formal commission or appointment to leadership position

**Mechanical Effect:** +2 Edge exercising institutional authority. Can issue orders that carry legal weight. Access to institutional resources.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Military officer or administrative official"
- Act 4: "Rebellion command structure member"
- Act 5: "Fleet officer with commission"

---

## Voice In the Council (Adept)

**Advancement Trigger:** Be consulted by high leadership on strategic decisions

**Mechanical Effect:** +2 Edge when your advice is sought. Your voice carries weight in councils. Can influence strategic direction.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Trusted advisor to leadership"
- Act 4: "Rebellion strategic council member"
- Act 5: "Fleet command advisory role"

---

## Mandate Holder (Expert)

**Advancement Trigger:** Receive formal mandate to act on behalf of higher authority

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge executing delegated authority. Can make binding decisions in defined scope. Your word IS the institution's word.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Royal mandate holder, speaks for throne"
- Act 4: "Rebellion plenipotentiary"
- Act 5: "Fleet authority in assigned theater"

---

## Chain-Shaper (Expert)

**Advancement Trigger:** Reform or establish new command structure that others adopt

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge designing institutional structures. Your organizational innovations become templates.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Reform military/administrative structures"
- Act 4: "Design rebellion command structure"
- Act 5: "Establish fleet organizational frameworks"

---

## Governor Of Many (Master)

**Advancement Trigger:** Hold authority over multiple administrative or military units simultaneously

**Mechanical Effect:** +3 Edge managing complex authority structures. Can coordinate multiple chains of command.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Regional governor or military commander"
- Act 4: "Rebellion multi-theater authority"
- Act 5: "Fleet sector command"

---

## Realm Regent / High Marshal (Apex)

**Advancement Trigger:** Your authority shapes how entire institutions function

**Mechanical Effect:** +4 Edge all exercises of institutional authority. You define what legitimate authority means in your sphere.

**Act Interpretations:**
- Act 2: "Second only to monarch in authority"
- Act 4: "Rebellion supreme authority figure"
- Act 5: "Fleet supreme command or civilian authority"

**DISCOVERS:** Authority is granted by institutions, but legitimacy comes from those who obey. The best authority figures serve those they command.
