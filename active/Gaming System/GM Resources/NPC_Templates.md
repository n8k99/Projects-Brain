# NPC Templates
## Quick Stat Blocks for Encounters

---

## How to Use These Templates

NPC templates provide **fast, functional stat blocks** for encounters. They're designed to:
- Be **memorable** in play (1-2 defining features)
- Support **quick resolution** (no complex abilities)
- Scale with **player power level**

### The Three Tiers

**Mooks**: Nameless threats, guards, bandits, minor obstacles
**Lieutenants**: Named NPCs, skilled opponents, minor villains
**Bosses**: Major antagonists, champions, climactic encounters

---

# Mook Templates

**Purpose**: Provide resistance without bogging down combat. Mooks go down quickly but can threaten through numbers.

**Design Philosophy**:
- Low HP (1-2 hits to drop)
- Simple attacks (no special abilities usually)
- Threatening in groups

---

## Combat Mooks

### Bandit / Thug

**Defense**: 12
**HP**: 10
**Attack**: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 damage (sword or club)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Cowardly (flees if outnumbered), Opportunistic

**Tactics**: Attack isolated targets, flee when losing

**Loot**: 3-10s, basic weapons, leather armor

---

### Town Guard

**Defense**: 13
**HP**: 12
**Attack**: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 damage (spear or shortsword)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Disciplined, Backup Available

**Tactics**: Call for reinforcements, hold positions, arrest rather than kill

**Loot**: 5s, guard uniform, spear, whistle

---

### Cultist / Zealot

**Defense**: 11
**HP**: 8
**Attack**: +2 to hit, 1d6 damage (dagger or ritual knife)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Fanatical (won't flee), Unarmed Combat

**Tactics**: Swarm targets, protect leader, sacrifice self if needed

**Loot**: Ritual robes, holy symbol, cryptic notes

---

### Soldier (Regular)

**Defense**: 14 (chain shirt + shield)
**HP**: 15
**Attack**: +4 to hit, 1d8+1 damage (longsword)
**Movement**: 25'/round (armored)

**Tags**: Formation Fighter (+1 Edge when adjacent to allies), Disciplined

**Tactics**: Fight in pairs, hold formation, follow orders

**Loot**: 10s, military gear, orders/documents

---

### Archer / Crossbowman

**Defense**: 12
**HP**: 10
**Attack**: +4 to hit, 1d8 damage (shortbow or light crossbow)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Ranged (60' effective range), Cowardly in melee

**Tactics**: Stay at range, target spellcasters/leaders, retreat if engaged in melee

**Loot**: 5s, bow or crossbow, 20 arrows/bolts

# Lieutenant Templates

**Purpose**: Challenging encounters for individuals or small groups. Lieutenants have 1-2 special abilities and can hold their own.

**Design Philosophy**:
- Moderate HP (3-5 hits to drop)
- 1-2 special abilities
- Tactical awareness

---

## Combat Lieutenants

### Veteran Soldier

**Defense**: 15 (scale mail + shield)
**HP**: 28
**Attack**: +5 to hit, 1d8+2 damage (longsword)
**Movement**: 25'/round

**Tags**: Combat Veteran, Shield Wall Expert

**Special Abilities**:
- **Shield Block**: Once per round, can impose -2 on attack against self or adjacent ally
- **Battle Cry**: Once per combat, grant all allies +1 Edge for one round

**Tactics**: Protect weaker allies, coordinate attacks, use terrain

**Loot**: 25s, quality weapons/armor, military decorations

---

### Assassin

**Defense**: 14
**HP**: 22
**Attack**: +6 to hit, 1d6+3 damage (poisoned dagger)
**Movement**: 35'/round

**Tags**: Stealth Expert, Poison User

**Special Abilities**:
- **Sneak Attack**: +2d6 damage when attacking from stealth/surprise
- **Vanish**: Can use action to become hidden if in shadows/cover (SEN 16 to spot)

**Tactics**: Strike from stealth, poison weapons, disengage if detected, strike again

**Loot**: 30s, masterwork daggers, poison vials, coded contract

---

### Battle Mage

**Defense**: 12 (magical wards)
**HP**: 24
**Attack**: +5 to hit, 2d6 fire damage (fire bolt, ranged 60')
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Monument-Trained, Magical Defenses

**Special Abilities**:
- **Shield Spell**: Reaction to gain +3 Defense against one attack (3/day)
- **Area Blast**: Action to hit all targets in 15' radius (2d6 damage, Difficulty 14 REF save for half) (2/day)

**Tactics**: Stay at range, use minions as shields, blast groups, shield spell against archers

**Loot**: 40s, spell components, Monument-forged focus, research notes

---

### Knight Champion

**Defense**: 17 (full plate + shield)
**HP**: 35
**Attack**: +6 to hit, 1d10+3 damage (longsword, versatile 2H)
**Movement**: 25'/round (heavily armored)

**Tags**: Noble Warrior, Duelist, Mounted (if on horse)

**Special Abilities**:
- **Weapon Master**: Can reroll one failed attack per round
- **Indomitable**: Once per combat, can ignore being reduced to 0 HP and remain at 1 HP instead

**Tactics**: Challenge strongest opponent, fight honorably (usually), protect innocents

**Loot**: 100s+, masterwork plate armor, noble signet, warhorse (if mounted)

## Social/Intrigue Lieutenants

### Master Spy

**Defense**: 13
**HP**: 20
**Attack**: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage (hidden blade)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Disguise Expert, Intelligence Network Access

**Special Abilities**:
- **Perfect Lie**: Once per conversation, automatically succeed on a Deception check

**Social**: CHM +4, SEN +5, MND +3

**Tactics**: Infiltrate, gather information, avoid combat, disappear if threatened

### Noble Diplomat

**Defense**: 11
**HP**: 18
**Attack**: +2 to hit, 1d6 damage (ceremonial rapier)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Political Authority, Noble Connections

**Special Abilities**:
- **Diplomatic Immunity**: Threats against them have political consequences
- **Rally Support**: Can call on 2d6 guards or allies within 1 hour in friendly territory

**Social**: CHM +5, MND +4

**Tactics**: Use words over weapons, call guards, threaten with political power, offer deals

**Information**: High-level political intelligence, treaty details, noble family secrets

**Loot**: 200s, noble regalia, important documents, signet ring

### Merchant Prince

**Defense**: 12
**HP**: 20
**Attack**: +3 to hit, 1d8+1 damage (hired bodyguard does the fighting)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Wealthy, Connected, Pragmatic

**Special Abilities**:
- **Bribe**: Can offer substantial payment to avoid conflict (100s+)
- **Trade Connections**: Can acquire almost any mundane item within a day

**Social**: CHM +4, MND +3

**Tactics**: Negotiate first, bribe second, call bodyguards third, flee if losing

**Information**: Trade routes, economic intelligence, black market contacts

**Loot**: 500s+, trade contracts, ledgers, rare goods

### Cult Leader

**Defense**: 13
**HP**: 25
**Attack**: +4 to hit, 1d8+2 damage (ritual staff)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Fanatical, Charismatic, Monument-Touched

**Special Abilities**:

**Social**: CHM +5, SPR +4

**Tactics**: Send cultists to die, use Monument powers, deliver monologues, bargain if losing

# Boss Templates

**Purpose**: Climactic encounters, major story antagonists, realm-shaking threats

**Design Philosophy**:
- High HP (6-10+ hits to drop)
- 3-4 special abilities
- Phase-based tactics (behavior changes as fight progresses)
- Memorable personality/presentation

---

## Combat Bosses

### Warlord General

**Defense**: 17 (masterwork full plate)
**HP**: 55
**Attack**: +8 to hit, 1d10+4 damage (greatsword, 2H)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Legendary Warrior, Army Commander, Tactical Genius

**Special Abilities**:
- **Command Presence**: All allied mooks within 30' gain +2 Edge
- **Cleave**: When killing an enemy, can immediately attack an adjacent enemy
- **Master of War**: Advantage on all combat rolls against targets he's observed fighting
- **Indomitable Will**: Immune to fear and charm effects

**Phase 1** (HP 55-30): Commands troops, fights defensively, analyzes opponents
**Phase 2** (HP 29-15): Dismisses troops, fights aggressively, uses terrain
**Phase 3** (HP 14-0): Berserk fury, reckless attacks, no defense

**Tactics**: Study opponents, coordinate troops, duel strongest enemy, refuse surrender

**Loot**: 500s+, legendary weapon, command seal, strategic maps

---

### Master Assassin

**Defense**: 16 (enchanted leather, magical wards)
**HP**: 45
**Attack**: +9 to hit, 1d6+4 damage + 2d6 poison (twin daggers)
**Movement**: 40'/round

**Tags**: Shadow Master, Poison Expert, Escape Artist

**Special Abilities**:
- **Death Strike**: Sneak attacks deal +4d6 damage instead of +2d6
- **Shadow Step**: Can teleport up to 30' to any shadow as bonus action (3/combat)
- **Smoke Bomb**: Create 15' radius smoke cloud, become invisible within it (1/combat)
- **Poison Mastery**: Immune to poison, can apply multiple poison types

**Phase 1** (HP 45-30): Hit and run from stealth, poison attacks
**Phase 2** (HP 29-15): Shadow step combat, aggressive strikes
**Phase 3** (HP 14-0): Desperate last stand or attempt escape

**Tactics**: Never fight fair, use environment, poison everything, escape if losing

**Loot**: 300s, masterwork weapons, rare poisons, contract details, intelligence

---

### Dragon Herald (Dragon Representative)

**Defense**: 18 (Monument-forged armor, magical)
**HP**: 60
**Attack**: +8 to hit, 2d6+3 damage (Monument-powered staff)
**Movement**: 30'/round, can levitate

**Tags**: Dragon Authority, Monument Master, Psychic Powers

**Special Abilities**:
- **Monument Control**: Can activate any Monument function within 100'
- **Psychic Assault**: Target makes SPR save (Difficulty 16) or takes 3d6 psychic damage and is stunned 1 round
- **Temporal Shift**: Can rewind time 6 seconds (negate one attack/action against self) (2/combat)
- **Dragon's Voice**: When speaking with Dragon authority, targets make SPR save (Difficulty 16) or must obey one command

**Phase 1** (HP 60-40): Attempts negotiation, demonstrates power
**Phase 2** (HP 39-20): Uses Monument functions aggressively, psychic attacks
**Phase 3** (HP 19-0): Calls Dragon assistance (fight ends, Dragons intervene)

**Tactics**: Negotiate first, demonstrate overwhelming power, call for Dragon backup

**Loot**: Monument-forged gear, Dragon communication device, restricted knowledge

## Social/Intrigue Bosses

**Defense**: 14
**HP**: 40
**Attack**: +6 to hit, 1d6+3 damage (hidden blade)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Intelligence Network Master, Blackmail Expert, Always Prepared

**Special Abilities**:
- **Know Your Secrets**: Has blackmail material on everyone (grants +4 Edge on all social rolls)

**Information**: Knows almost everything happening in the realm, has files on everyone important

**Loot**: Coded intelligence archives (reveals many secrets), contact networks, blackmail material

**Defense**: 13 (magical wards)
**HP**: 48
**Attack**: +5 to hit, 2d8 damage (Monument-tech weapon)
**Movement**: 30'/round

**Tags**: Ancient Knowledge, Monument Theorist, Chronicle Keeper

## Monster Bosses

### Ancient Dracolich

**Defense**: 19 (ancient scales, magical wards)
**HP**: 80
**Attack**: +10 to hit, 2d10+5 damage (bite) or 3d6 necrotic (death breath, 30' cone)
**Movement**: 30'/round walking, 60'/round flying

**Tags**: Undead Dragon, Ancient, Magical

**Special Abilities**:
- **Frightful Presence**: All who see it make SPR save (Difficulty 18) or flee in terror
- **Death Breath**: 30' cone, 3d6 necrotic damage, WIL save (Difficulty 16) or paralyzed 1 minute (Recharge 5-6 on d6)

**Phase 2** (HP 49-25): Lands and melee, uses frightful presence
**Phase 3** (HP 24-0): Desperate fury, breath weapon spam

**Tactics**: Fly and strafe, breath weapon groups, land to finish wounded, flee if truly threatened

**Loot**: Ancient hoard (5,000s+ treasure), artifacts, Chronicle fragments

### Monument Aberration

**Defense**: 16 (crystalline shell)
**HP**: 70
**Attack**: +7 to hit, 2d8+3 damage (energy tendril)
**Movement**: 20'/round, can teleport short distances

**Tags**: Monument Malfunction, Reality Warper, Unpredictable

**Special Abilities**:
- **Spatial Distortion**: Area within 30' has warped physics (movement halved, disorientation)

# Quick NPC Generation

## Five-Minute NPC Creation

When you need an NPC fast:

1. **Choose Tier**: Mook / Lieutenant / Boss
2. **Choose Type**: Combat / Social / Monster
3. **Pick Template** from above
4. **Add 1-2 Personal Touches**:
   - Name
   - Visual detail (scar, accent, mannerism)
   - Goal or secret
   - Connection to players

**Example**:
- **Template**: Veteran Soldier (Lieutenant)
- **Name**: Captain Marra Kelleson
- **Detail**: Missing left ear from old battle
- **Goal**: Retire after one more campaign
- **Secret**: Owes money to Silent Registry
- **Connection**: Trained one of the PCs

---

## Scaling NPCs to Party Level

### Low-Tier Party (Novice-Journeyman PCs)
Use mooks and lieutenants as written. Bosses should be rare, multi-session builds.

### Mid-Tier Party (Adept-Expert PCs)
- Mooks: Use as written, increase numbers
- Lieutenants: Add +5 HP, +1 to hit, +1 damage
- Bosses: Use as written

### High-Tier Party (Master-Apex PCs)
- Mooks: Not threatening unless in huge numbers
- Lieutenants: Add +10 HP, +2 to hit, +2 damage, one extra ability
- Bosses: Add +15 HP, +2 to hit, +3 damage, one extra ability

---

# Named NPC Framework

For important recurring NPCs, define:

1. **Goal**: What they want to achieve
2. **Method**: How they pursue it (honorably, ruthlessly, cautiously, etc.)
3. **Secret**: What they're hiding
4. **Resources**: What they control (wealth, troops, information, magic)
5. **Connection**: How they tie to the PCs (ally, rival, mentor, enemy)
6. **Stats**: Use template as baseline, modify as needed
